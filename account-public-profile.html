Mezcal – the agave spirit better known as tequila's cooler and smokier cousin – has experienced a lot of hype over the past few years.

Production numbers are soaring to new heights and more and more people outside of Mexico are discovering this diverse and exciting liquor that has an incredible range of taste notes. From smokey to sweet, from citrus to clay and from cinnamon to pepper, there is a whole world for your taste buds to discover.
