Products Classification

We collaborate with multiple suppliers from around the world to bring you these high-quality swords that cater to customers with different needs and budgets. Our products are categorized into four main categories:

Practice Swords
$80 Or Less
Most of these products are crafted from materials like wood and bamboo for training purposes, as well as plastic for LARPing activities. Additionally, this category includes toy swords. Items in this range are usually priced under $80.
Casual Swords
$80 to $200
These products are priced somewhere between $80 and $200 and are made from regular metals for decorations and collections. They offer great value for money and are perfect for customers looking for quality swords at affordable prices.
Superior Swords
$200 to $900
Similar to the previous category but they are made with a bit more attention or more high-quality metals are used in comparison with the Casual swords, which makes them perfect for cutting and thrusting. These products are somewhere between $200 and $399.
Authentic Swords
$400 to $2500
These products are carefully crafted and produced by experienced swordsmiths to create authentic replicas of historical swords. These swords are very popular among passionate collectors but not only. Their price can start from $400 and go up to $2500 in rare cases.

We've addressed the price ranges for each classification, but the price isn't the sole criterion for categorizing our products.

With numerous suppliers offering diverse price points, you may occasionally find authentic swords of comparable quality to superior models.

Our product classifications are dynamic and regularly updated, taking into account factors such as reviews, quality, and brand reputation.

We are always working to improve our product portfolio and expand our variety of swords or the range of suppliers. To keep up with our latest updates and collaborations, please visit our brand page.

Shipping Policy

We have products that are shipped from either our warehouse in the US or from our international suppliers, as we find it hard to keep stock of all product variations. After receiving feedback from our customers, we have implemented the following delivery scenarios:

1 Ordering products from US Suppliers ONLY:

Products will be delivered in 2-3 working days once the order is processed.

2 Ordering products from International Suppliers ONLY:

We partner with different companies to inspect and bundle the products in the same location as the supplier, so they can be shipped directly to you.

Our standard delivery time for most products is 2-3 weeks. Nonetheless, if you wish to order items that are not currently in stock or require custom orders, these may take approximately 1-2 months to be fulfilled.

3 Ordering products from BOTH US and International Suppliers:

You will receive separate shipments as soon as the products are ready. We will cover the cost of the second shipping. Previously, we waited for all products to arrive at our warehouse, inspect and bundle them before sending them to customers.

However, this slightly extended delivery time. To ensure faster delivery times, we have implemented this new policy as we continue to grow our inventory to be able to keep stock of most products and ship them more efficiently.

The estimated shipping time and costs would be shown at the checkout page before making the payment.

We are committed to ensuring that our customers receive their orders as quickly and efficiently as possible.

Tracking Orders and Shipping Companies

As we collaborate with multiple brands and suppliers worldwide, we use different shipping companies to ensure fast and efficient delivery. For products shipped from our warehouse in the US, we rely on UPS and FedEx.

For products shipped from other suppliers, we use a mix of international and local shipping companies to expedite the shipping process and ensure cost-efficient delivery, which may be reflected in the final price of the product.

Once your order has been processed and handed over to the shipping company, we will send you a tracking code so that you can track your order throughout the shipping process.

Returns Policy

We understand that returning swords can be challenging due to many factors. If you are unsatisfied with an item for any reason, you may return it to us within 30 days from the date of receiving the order for a full refund, minus shipping costs.

While it is rare, if you receive a custom-made sword with quality issues or parts that require changes due to small errors or wrong specifications, and you are able to prove it, we will replace the sword for you. Once an order has been placed and processed, it cannot be canceled.

Please note that there are exceptions to our refund policy. We cannot accept returns for items that were on sale or custom-made swords based on the client's requests.
